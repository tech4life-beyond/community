# Reputation & Credits System

**Tech4Life & Beyond LLC**  
**Applies to:** All contributors within the Tech4Life ecosystem

---

## 1. Purpose

Tech4Life does not use salaries as primary motivation.

Instead, it operates on:
- Contribution-based recognition
- Reputation accumulation
- Royalty-linked credit allocation
- Public authorship acknowledgment

Reputation is earned through value creation, not titles.

---

## 2. What Is Reputation?

Reputation is a documented measure of:
- Technical contribution
- Ethical consistency
- Documentation discipline
- Leadership quality
- Long-term reliability

Reputation is visible within the ecosystem and influences:
- Role elevation
- Product Cell leadership eligibility
- Royalty allocation weighting

---

## 3. Contribution Credits

Each contribution is logged and categorized as:

- Concept Design
- Technical Architecture
- Engineering Work
- Research & Validation
- Ethical Review
- Documentation
- Licensing Support

Each category may carry weighted credit depending on impact and complexity.

---

## 4. Credit Scoring Model (Framework Level)

Credits are assigned based on:
- Effort (time & depth)
- Impact (strategic importance)
- Uniqueness (originality)
- Execution quality

Credit scoring must be:
- Transparent
- Documented
- Approved within the Product Cell

No retroactive credit manipulation is allowed.

---

## 5. Royalty Allocation Link

When a product generates revenue:

Royalty distribution within the Product Cell is based on:
- Documented contribution weight
- Agreed internal distribution model
- Governance confirmation

Reputation does not override documented contribution.

---

## 6. Public Recognition

Each product will include:
- Creator credits
- Contributor list
- Architect acknowledgment

Tech4Life preserves historical authorship permanently.

---

## 7. Reputation Advancement

Higher reputation may allow:
- Advancement to Creator level
- Advancement to Architect level
- Participation in governance discussions

Reputation decays only through misconduct or inactivity.

---

## 8. Integrity Safeguards

Reputation cannot be:
- Purchased
- Transferred
- Manipulated privately

All reputation data must be verifiable through documented contributions.

---

## 9. Ethical Foundation

Reputation without ethics is corruption.

Tech4Life prioritizes:
- Integrity over speed
- Contribution over charisma
- Documentation over claims

---

**End of Reputation & Credits System Framework**
