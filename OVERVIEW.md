## 1. What is Tech4Life & Beyond?
Tech4Life & Beyond is an open ecosystem for designing, building, and sustaining life-improving products. It connects people, tools, and licenses so that solutions can be created, shared, and manufactured responsibly across regions.

## 2. The Problem We Solve
Many high-impact products are hard to access, hard to manufacture locally, or locked behind closed designs. This ecosystem focuses on:
- Translating proven ideas into buildable, local-ready solutions.
- Reducing duplication by sharing a common registry and licenses.
- Supporting equitable participation across builders, contributors, and manufacturers.

## 3. The Open Impact Model (TOIL explained simply)
TOIL (Tools of Impact License) is an open license that protects the intent of public-good designs. In simple terms:
- **Open by default**: Designs and documentation are shareable and modifiable.
- **Impact-aligned**: Use and distribution must support the social benefit purpose.
- **Traceable**: The origin and changes are recorded to keep the chain of impact clear.

## 5. Current Live Components
- **Products**: The practical outputs designed for real-world use.
- **Kivai Platform**: The collaboration and lifecycle system for building and improving products.
- **TOIL License**: The legal framework that keeps the ecosystem open and impact-aligned.
- **Product Registry**: The shared source of truth for product definitions and versions.

## 6. How to Participate
- **Builder**: Create or adapt products and publish improvements.
- **Contributor**: Provide research, documentation, testing, or localization.
- **Manufacturer**: Produce validated products locally with quality and feedback loops.
- **Educator**: Train others to design, build, or use the products effectively.

## 7. Road to Global Impact (long-term vision)
The long-term aim is a resilient, globally distributed network where high-impact products can be built anywhere, improved continuously, and governed transparently. Success looks like:
- A growing catalog of locally buildable, proven solutions.
- Regional manufacturing and repair capacity.
- Shared standards that reduce barriers and increase trust.
- A feedback-driven loop that keeps products relevant and effective.
