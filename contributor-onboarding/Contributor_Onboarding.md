# Contributor Onboarding Framework

**Tech4Life & Beyond LLC**  
**Applies to:** All contributors, collaborators, and participants within the Tech4Life ecosystem

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines how individuals enter, integrate, and evolve within Tech4Life & Beyond.

The onboarding process ensures:
- Alignment with Tech4Life values
- Ethical understanding of Tech4Life Open Impact License (TOIL) v1.0
- Clarity of expectations
- Structured access to projects and repositories

Onboarding is not a hiring process. It is an alignment process.

---

## 2. Contributor Philosophy

Tech4Life operates under:
- Horizontal leadership
- Distributed responsibility
- Contribution-based recognition
- Royalty-based compensation (not salaries)

Participation is voluntary and purpose-driven.

---

## 3. Entry Levels

All participants enter at the **Observer Level**.

### Level 1 — Observer
- Can read public documentation
- May suggest ideas
- No repository write access

### Level 2 — Contributor
- Approved by governance review
- May join project cells
- Limited write access
- Signs Mutual NDA if required

### Level 3 — Creator
- Actively develops product concepts
- Participates in design and documentation
- Eligible for royalty participation

### Level 4 — Architect
- Leads product cells
- Guides governance decisions
- Oversees ethical compliance within projects

Advancement is based on contribution quality, not time.

---

## 4. Onboarding Steps

1. Public review of Tech4Life Foundation
2. Agreement with Tech4Life Open Impact License (TOIL) v1.0 principles
3. Initial idea submission or expression of interest
4. Ethics alignment acknowledgment
5. Assignment to Observer level
6. Progressive elevation through contribution

---

## 5. Documentation Requirements

All contributors must:
- Use official templates
- Document contributions transparently
- Respect Tech4Life Open Impact License (TOIL) v1.0 attribution standards
- Maintain professional conduct

---

## 6. Compensation Awareness

Contributors understand that:
- No salary is provided
- Compensation occurs only if a product generates revenue
- Royalty distribution follows TLOS and Royalty Framework rules

Participation does not guarantee financial return.

---

## 7. Removal & Suspension

A contributor may be removed if:
- Ethical violations occur
- Confidentiality is breached
- Governance rules are repeatedly ignored

Removal does not erase prior documented contributions.

---

## 8. Alignment Statement

By participating in Tech4Life, contributors acknowledge:
- Technology must not harm humanity or the planet
- Collaboration outweighs ego
- Documentation equals accountability

---

**End of Contributor Onboarding Framework**
